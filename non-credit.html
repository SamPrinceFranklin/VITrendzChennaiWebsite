Arignar Anna Tamil Mandram Arignar Anna Tamil Mandram aims at spreading the Tamil language and its rich culture to young minds. Tamil Mandram organizing events like poem recital, article writing and debates on various topics related to the language. The “pongal” vizha is a signature event for Tamil Mandram. It will be celebrated every year with its traditional values. Maakkolam, Uri adiththal, Bullock ride and Silambam will be demonstrated by the students during Pongal Vizha. The traditional sports like kho-kho and kabbadi will be conducted for girls and boys respectively. A cultural program will be organized on the eve of pongal. Aringar Anna Tamil Mandram will be organizing classes to learn silambam.

Aerodynamics Club Aerodynamics is the way air moves around things. The rules of aerodynamics explain how an airplane is able to fly. Anything that moves through air reacts to aerodynamics. A rocket blasting off the launch pad and a kite in the sky react to aerodynamics. Aerodynamics even acts on cars, since air flows around cars.This club lets you get familiarised and helps you in learning the concepts

EUREKA Physics Club This club’s core activities will be focused on learning and doing physics to enhance our understanding of physics in a variety of settings. Starting our exploration of physical phenomena in the seemingly mundane instances from our daily lives we are prepared to explore the profound and breath-taking phenomena occurring in the cosmos that puzzle us in a larger than life setting. The club will focus on fun filled science activities such as astronomy with sky-watch observatory visits, problem-solving sessions, lectures and talks to get insights from great physicists and hands-on research opportunities.
