Arignar Anna Tamil Mandram Arignar Anna Tamil Mandram aims at spreading the Tamil language and its rich culture to young minds. Tamil Mandram organizing events like poem recital, article writing and debates on various topics related to the language. The “pongal” vizha is a signature event for Tamil Mandram. It will be celebrated every year with its traditional values. Maakkolam, Uri adiththal, Bullock ride and Silambam will be demonstrated by the students during Pongal Vizha. The traditional sports like kho-kho and kabbadi will be conducted for girls and boys respectively. A cultural program will be organized on the eve of pongal. Aringar Anna Tamil Mandram will be organizing classes to learn silambam.

Entrepreneur start-ups or lean start-ups are businesses with humble beginning but with great promising features. These help the younger generation on becoming self-dependent and be more creative in their respective fields of interest. Even Indian government has taken initiatives to promote entrepreneurship and creativity in people with schemes such as Startup India campaign and making India campaign. National entrepreneurship network is an initiative take by VIT Chennai campus to promote the Startup India campaign. It aims at providing proper environment to promote and incubate the ideas and creativity of students. The club provides all necessary theoretical and practical help which is necessary for carrying out their early business plans. This is the best opportunity for students to try, fail and try again and collect both experience and knowledge on humble level before stepping into the competitive world.
