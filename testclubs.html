Designer Club intends to integrate inter-discipline minds to nurture ideas and engineer them into products. Club members are encouraged to brainstorm ideas using concept development groups (CDG) and the team identifies the skills required to engineer those concepts. Thus one of the major objectives would be to have training for dummies across the necessary engineering domains. Further there is always an expert lecture series on intellectual property rights and patenting part of the activities. This club is many times misconstrued with art & graphic design , which is not and is more of industrial design ! The various activities include CDGs conceiving ideas in structured brainstorming sessions, skill training sessions for dummies and also encourages to participate in inter-collegiate design contests. There had been interactions from industrial styling experts, concept designers etc. too in the past. Few developed concepts include innovative home appliances, medical instruments, table top holders etc. Interestingly computer engineering students have learnt CAD to mechanical simulation and mechanical students had sessions on embedded tools. This makes the club versatile for any disciple engineering student to nurture and bring their ideas to reality!

Pals is an IIT Alumni initiative dedicated to bridging the gap between industry requirements and theoretical aspects of academics. Our aim at PALS is to ensure effective and seamless application of the skills one learns in closed classrooms and textbooks. Rotary International is an international service organization whose stated purpose is to bring together business and professional leaders in order to provide humanitarian services, encourage high ethical standards in all vocations, and help build goodwill and peace in the world. It is a secular organization open to all persons regardless of race, color, creed, religion, gender, or political preference. There are 34,282 clubs and over 1.2 million members worldwide. THE ROTARACT CLUB of VIT University Chennai, an initiative of The Rotary International, is a service club for students of VIT to organize service oriented activities, develop leadership and professional skills, and socialize amongst peers.

“Empowering Youth-Fueling Change” is the motto the members of Uddeshya abide by on their journey to achieve a long lasting change. It is a youth based NGO, founded by a group of enthusiastic students of VIT University, which now functions at a national level.

It was expanded to various parts of the country. After functioning for a time period of one year, and making an outreach to over 8 states in the nation, Uddeshya has finally been able to successfully establish itself in VIT.

Our goal is to reach out to the youth on a peer to peer approach ensuring them to be capable of handling the challenges and becoming the ambassadors of change. We train members as peer educators and leaders wherein encouragement to identify and tackle problems such as bullying, discrimination, substance abuse and sex education is given.

Our uniqueness lies in the fact that we utilize positive peer pressure as a tool of guidance, inspiration and encouragement enabling us to set the wheels of change in motion. We strive to provide a platform for budding entrepreneurs in concurrence with bettering the society and themselves.

VITeach is a student run social club at VIT University, Chennai campus. Students from different branches and batches come together to teach the children of Aadhi Dravida Nada Middle School, a government school in Melakottiyur. The children are taught spoken and written English through engaging, interactive and colorful mediums. The club members also organize ‘Sunday Kids’ sessions for the children of the workers in the campus. At these sessions the children are encouraged to involve in games incorporating the ‘Play and Learn’ technique. Additionally, over the course of the academic year, events such as Kids Carnival, Wishtree and plays are conducted for the children.

We, a group of 50 students and 1 faculty, embark ourselves in amazing, spine-chilling and ecstatic treks, which ultimately gets drilled in our minds and hearts as memorable events with unparalleled experiences.
