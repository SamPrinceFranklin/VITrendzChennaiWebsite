FFCS

Fully Flexible Credit System. You can choose the courses you want to learn this semester. You will also be choosing the faculty/time slot of the class along with the venue. .accordion-body

In the wishlist the students participate and tell VIT what subjects students are looking for. It can be said as a kind of survey that VIT does to know that which subject is required, by how many students. So lets say more students want to do a course (eg: XYZ), it will provide more faculties for the course XYZ. First wishlist is done and then the actual allocation is done. .accordion-body

Mock FFCS is a kind of practice for actual FFCS. Mock won't affect actual FFCS. It allows to understand how the system works? faculties and demand of faculties. FFCS happens after the mock FFCS and they can choose PE, PC, UC and UE they are going to do in that semester. STS and non-credit courses will be given in FFCS and students will have to choose directly. .accordion-body

GRADING

Grade depends on the mean and the standard deviation of the class. Most of the courses in VIT are graded by relative grading. .accordion-body

Grade won't depend on the marks of your class members. .accordion-body

GPA- Grade Point Average CGPA- Cummulative Grade Point Average. GPA is calculated at the end of every semester. CGPA is the new average that is taken out on the basis of your new GPA. To sum up basically CGPA denotes your performance on the whole including all the semester whereas GPA denotes your performance on one semester. .accordion-body
