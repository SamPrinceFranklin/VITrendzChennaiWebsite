What is VITrendz?

VITrendz is cofounded by a group of individuals who strive to make lives at VIT easier. It is an organization that helps students at VIT by providing them various services and academic resources. It provides entertainment, study materials, interactive help and a vast stream of other facilities to VITians based on their changing requirements. Having said all this, what make VITrendz one of its kind is that it focuses on the betterment and improvement in all the possible fields and that it is built by the motive of providing all sorts of help digitally (through Videos on Youtube, Social Media, Drives and its very own website).

Wanna Know us Better?

Vision

Our Vision is to make college a place that is easier to survive and concentrate on things that are important and not that are a compulsion on the students. The main motive is to remind the students that there is a little more to life than just studies, so they dont get too involved to be emotionally disturbed because of their performance. So as to implement this, we serve entertainment and education to the students in equity. And we tend to do this for all the engineering student who feel left out in their college life.

Message & Motto

We here at VITrendz work and support ethically and we expect students of VIT to use our resources in the same way for their good will. We, hereby, declare that we do not indulge in illegal activities against the university’s code of conduct and won’t encourage any such things that are against the college’s reputation and dignity and we request supporters and users of VITrendz amenities to not misuse any of our work towards your betterment. We only request you to share your happiness and love for our work in making your college lives easier and memorable. VITrendz is nothing without the love showered from its supporters and users.

How do we work?

All members of VITrendz come from different backgrounds with different talents. Everyone in VITrendz is a talented artist in their own way. While we are serious about our work to make students’ lives in VIT better, but we work efficiently as a family. We are highly dedicated towards our work with utmost selfless dignity and focus. but we also dearly look after our family members’ backs and give the extra boost to make our organization distinctively better and eventually, the best. The laws of equity are highly respected and obeyed by every member and every member gets a chance to bloom to their glory. We are like-minded people who also love to push boundaries with out-of-the-box ideas to make at least one student’s life better.
